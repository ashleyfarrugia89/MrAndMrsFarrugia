Where is it?

The Venue

We are so excited to be celebrating our wedding at the stunning Peckforton Castle — a beautiful Victorian Gothic castle set in the heart of the Cheshire countryside. We can't wait to share this magical place with you.

Getting There

The castle is easily accessible by car and has ample on-site parking. Taxi and ride-share services operate locally from Crewe and Chester stations.

By Train

The nearest train stations are Crewe (approx. 20 min by taxi) and Chester (approx. 25 min by taxi).

Good to Know

The castle grounds include a chapel and beautiful gardens. Please wear sensible footwear if you plan to explore — the grounds can be uneven.
